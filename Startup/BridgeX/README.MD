# BridgeX

**Founder:** Temitayo Adebisi

BridgeX is a crypto-to-investment bridge that enables users to access Nigerian fixed-income products such as Treasury Bills, government bonds, money market funds, and mutual funds using stablecoins like USDT and USDC instead of traditional Naira bank transfers.[1]

## Overview

The project is built around a simple idea: make it easier for diaspora Nigerians, local crypto holders, and cross-border investors to move from digital assets into regulated Nigerian investment products without needing a local bank account.[1]

BridgeX connects crypto payment rails with licensed Nigerian financial institutions, handling stablecoin deposits, conversion into Naira through regulated partners, investment execution through brokers or asset managers, and digital proof of ownership for the investor.[1]

## Why it matters

Today, access to many Nigerian fixed-income and fund products is tied to local banking infrastructure, which creates friction for users who already operate primarily with stablecoins.[1]

BridgeX is designed to remove that friction by offering a clearer path from crypto holdings to safer, yield-focused investment products in a regulated local market.[1]

## Core idea

At a high level, users deposit stablecoins, the funds are converted into Naira through approved partners, the investment is placed with a licensed institution, and the user receives a digital record of their holdings, with room for future tokenized receipts.[1]
